Our Mission

Vessel World is working with young people to promote access to internet technology and digital content. We aim to impact one million young people in Africa over the next 5 years. Vessel World is a young people centered non-profit organization registered in the United States, working with and through young people, promoting internet access and content as a platform for sustained development among young people in Africa. Our vision is a better world for all, served by an empowered and committed community of young people.

What We Do

  • Promote access to internet connected devices: We work with schools and other organizations in the U.S. to collect smartphones, tablets, and laptops, and deliver them to young people in Africa.
  • Promote access to internet content for education and life skills: We aggregate content and create our own content aimed at minimizing the digital gap for young people in Africa.

How You Can Help
